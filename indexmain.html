Build and Race (hardcoded)


Row, row, row your boat, and see if you can make your cardboard boat float! Teams are to make use of the cardboard materials and construct a seaworthy boat. As a finale, teams will take their boats and race against one another, either in a pool or the open sea! This is a challenge where your participants will experience the “impossible” become achievable! This team building challenge focuses on creativity, innovation and communication among the participants.

The Royal Chariot(hardcoded)


Racing against the clock and each other, teams are required to pull together in this inspiring Royal Chariot challenge which combines the skills of creativity, innovation and communication. Teams will need to transform their cardboard materials into a life-sized racing chariot, where machine and human-power will work together in harmony. This Royal Chariot team building challenge will conclude with the Chariot of Fire, where teams will pull their teammate in their chariots, and race against their opponents.
